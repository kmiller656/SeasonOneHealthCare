Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Important Notice — Our Two Services

Season One Healthcare operates two related services covered by this policy: (1) a PA placement service that researches and presents job opportunities to Physician Assistants and facilities, and (2) a PA contact database that gives healthcare recruiters and staffing firms subscription access to verified Physician Assistant professional contact information. We are not a healthcare provider and do not collect or process protected health information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA in either service.

For the placement service, Season One Healthcare is not a licensed employment agency, talent agency, or legal agent under applicable state or federal law. See our Terms of Service for the full disclaimer governing that relationship.

This Privacy Policy describes how Season One Healthcare ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects information about you when you use our website, subscribe to our PA opportunity list, register for the PA contact database, or contact us. By using either service or submitting your information, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you subscribe, register, submit a form, or contact us, we collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name and email address
  • For PAs: professional credential (PA-C or PA Student), specialty, practice setting, location, compensation goals, and availability
  • For recruiter accounts: company name and role, plus billing information (processed by Stripe — we do not store payment card data)
  • Any additional information you choose to share

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our website or app, we may automatically collect limited technical information including IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, referring URLs, and session activity.

PA Professional Contact Data

Our PA contact database contains professional information gathered from publicly available sources (state licensing boards, professional associations, and the National Plan and Provider Enumeration System (NPPES), a public federal registry maintained by the Centers for Medicare & Medicaid Services) as well as information voluntarily submitted through our forms. A record may include: name, work email, deliverability/verification status, professional or student membership type, city, state, practice or employer organization, clinical specialty, and NPI. This is public professional directory information, not PHI. See our Data Methodology page for full sourcing details. Any PA may request removal at any time via our Data Removal Request page.

A PA who registers for our placement service may separately opt in to also appear in the recruiter-facing contact database — this only happens with an explicit, affirmative choice on their part, never automatically.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information we collect to:

  • Research and identify PA job opportunities, contracts, and direct hire positions that match your profile
  • Send you curated opportunity updates and announcements
  • Connect you with healthcare facilities that are actively seeking PA candidates
  • Create and manage recruiter accounts, process payments, and provide access to the PA contact database
  • Send transactional communications (account confirmations, billing receipts, support responses)
  • Respond to inquiries and support requests, and improve our services
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties or use it for advertising. You may withdraw consent at any time by unsubscribing.

3. Data Sharing

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Healthcare Facilities: With your explicit consent, we may share a PA's professional profile with facilities seeking to hire. We will always notify a PA before making an introduction.
  • Recruiter Subscribers: PA contact records in our database (sourced publicly, or added via explicit PA opt-in) are made available to paying recruiter subscribers for professional recruiting outreach, subject to the obligations in our Terms of Service.
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors that help us operate the service (Supabase for database hosting, Stripe for payment processing, form processing, and email delivery). These providers are contractually required to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with appropriate notice to you.

4. Data Security & Standards

Encryption in Transit & at Rest

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher (HTTPS). Stored data is protected using AES-256 encryption through our database provider.

Access Controls

Access to personal data is restricted on a need-to-know basis with authentication controls and row-level security enforced at the database layer.

CAN-SPAM Compliance

All marketing emails include a clear unsubscribe link and comply with the CAN-SPAM Act. Recruiter subscribers are responsible for ensuring their own outreach to PA contacts complies with CAN-SPAM and applicable state laws.

Minimal Data Collection

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services. We do not collect SSNs, passport numbers, or sensitive financial data.

No method of transmission or storage is completely secure. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately.

5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@seasononehealthcare.com.

AccessRequest a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
CorrectionRequest correction of any inaccurate personal information we have on file.
DeletionRequest deletion of your personal information, subject to any legal retention obligations.
Opt-OutUnsubscribe from PA communications any time via our unsubscribe page, or request removal from the PA contact database via our Data Removal Request page.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as you remain subscribed, your recruiter account is active, or as needed to provide our services. If you unsubscribe, cancel, or request deletion, we will remove your personal information within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

PA professional contact data is reviewed on an ongoing basis. Any PA may request removal at any time via our Data Removal Request page.

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for basic site functionality and authentication. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or cross-site behavioral analytics. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For material changes, we will provide notice via email or a prominent announcement on our website.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@seasononehealthcare.com
  • Website: seasononehealthcare.com

We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
