The PA Job Market by the Numbers

Real data from verified sources — so you know exactly what the market looks like and what you should be earning.

Data Disclaimer: All compensation figures and market statistics on this page are sourced from publicly available third-party reports (AAPA, BLS, Medscape, AMN Healthcare, Barton Associates) and are provided for general informational purposes only. These figures represent national or regional averages and ranges — actual compensation varies by specialty, experience, location, and facility. Season One Healthcare does not guarantee any specific compensation outcome.

Where You Work Matters

  • Locum / Contract (all specialties) — $141k–$280k+/yr. Source: AMN Healthcare, 2025–2026
  • Emergency Medicine — $146k median/yr. Source: AAPA 2025 Salary Report
  • Orthopedic Surgery — $135k median/yr. Source: AAPA 2025 Salary Report
  • Family Medicine — $125k median/yr. Source: AAPA 2025 Salary Report
  • National Median (all settings) — $134k/yr. Source: AAPA 2025 Salary Report

Locum tenens PAs typically earn 20–30% more per hour than equivalent permanent employees, plus housing stipends and travel reimbursement on many assignments.

NHSC Loan Repayment Eligibility

Many facility types we source from — FQHCs, RHCs, CAHs, and Tribal Health programs — qualify for National Health Service Corps loan repayment. Our team flags these for eligible PAs. Eligibility requirements apply; visit HRSA.gov for current program details.

5-Year Compensation Growth

AAPA data shows consistent median compensation growth: $111k (2020) → $115k (2021) → $120k (2022) → $127k (2023) → $134k (2024) → $140k (2025). A 26% increase over five years.

The Case for an Agent

PAs who work with a dedicated agent spend less time searching and land positions faster than those who go it alone — with a team vetting facilities and handling outreach on their behalf.
