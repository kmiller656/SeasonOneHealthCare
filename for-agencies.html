Our Difference

Our Agency plan is built for teams, not individual seats. Your recruiters share a pooled credit balance, search the same verified 60,000+ PA database, and export directly into your existing ATS or CRM — no per-recruiter licensing, no long onboarding. Every contact is verified before it's revealed, so your team spends its time on outreach instead of chasing bounced emails.

4 Steps to Scaling Your Team's PA Pipeline

  1. Add your recruiters to a shared Agency plan with pooled credits.
  2. Set state and specialty filters so each recruiter sees the right slice of the database.
  3. Export verified contacts directly into your team's ATS or CRM.
  4. Track newly funded rural and underserved facilities with Grant Tracker — surface warm leads with a confirmed staffing mandate before your competitors do.
