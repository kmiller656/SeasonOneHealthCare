2. Description of Services

Season One Healthcare provides the following services to Physician Assistants (PA-Cs) and PA students at no charge:

  • Research and identification of PA employment opportunities, including permanent positions, locum tenens contracts, and direct hire roles available across the United States
  • Curated delivery of relevant opportunities based on your stated preferences and profile
  • Introduction and connection between qualified PA candidates and healthcare facilities with open positions
  • General market information related to PA compensation, practice settings, and opportunity availability

To healthcare facilities, Season One Healthcare provides candidate sourcing and introduction services for a placement fee paid by the facility upon successful hire. PAs never pay for these services.

Separately, Season One Healthcare provides healthcare recruiters and staffing firms with subscription access to a verified database of Physician Assistant professional contact information, searchable and exportable for professional recruiting and staffing outreach.

All services are provided on an "as available" basis. We do not guarantee uninterrupted access, placement, or the accuracy of any specific opportunity or contact record.

3. Placement Service — Not a Licensed Agent

Season One Healthcare operates as a healthcare recruitment and placement company. As part of this role, our team identifies qualified PA candidates, presents opportunities to both candidates and facilities, coordinates placement discussions, assists with the preparation and presentation of employment contracts and placement agreements between facilities and candidates, and may assist with credentialing documentation and verification. This describes our operational function as a recruiter — it does not create a legal agency relationship, fiduciary duty, or power of attorney.

Specifically, Season One Healthcare:

  • Is a recruitment and placement company — not a licensed employment agency under RCW 19.31 (Washington State) or any equivalent state statute
  • Does not have legal authority to sign, execute, or bind contracts on behalf of either the PA or the facility
  • Does not provide legal representation or legal advice of any kind
  • Does not guarantee any specific outcome, offer, compensation, or placement
  • Is not a party to any employment agreement between a PA and a facility — we facilitate and present, we do not sign or execute

All final employment decisions, contract negotiations, offer acceptances, and binding agreements are made directly and solely between the PA and the hiring facility. If you require legal counsel in connection with any employment matter, you should consult a licensed attorney.

For PA Contact Database Subscribers: Subscriptions are billed monthly or annually as selected at signup, at the prices listed at the time of purchase. Monthly plans may be cancelled at any time with no cancellation fee; annual plans may be cancelled with access retained through the end of the billing period, without prorated refunds.
