Season One Healthcare was built around a simple belief: every Physician Assistant deserves a dedicated team working in their corner — not a job board, not a recruiter working for the facility, but a team working entirely for them.

Our Story

Built to Represent You

The PA job market is full of job boards, generic recruiters, and platforms built to serve facilities — not clinicians. PAs spend dozens of hours searching, applying, and negotiating alone, while the facilities they're applying to have HR departments, compensation consultants, and legal teams working on their side.

Season One Healthcare was founded to change that dynamic. Our agents work on your behalf — sourcing opportunities, vetting facilities, and identifying the highest-paying contracts and direct hire positions available across the United States so you don't have to go looking for them.

The name reflects where we are: this is our first season. We're building something deliberately, one PA and one placement at a time, with a commitment to always keeping our service free for the clinicians we represent.

Facilities We Source From

We know where
the best opportunities are

Our agents focus on facility types that offer strong compensation, broad PA scope, and meaningful clinical work.

01

Critical Access Hospitals

Rural CAHs frequently offer premium compensation packages to attract qualified PAs. Our team knows which facilities are actively recruiting and what they're willing to pay.

02

Federally Qualified Health Centers

FQHCs offer competitive salaries, NHSC loan repayment eligibility, and broad clinical scope. Our agents identify mission-aligned opportunities that also pay well.

03

Rural Health Clinics

RHCs operate in designated shortage areas and frequently offer recruitment bonuses and above-market salaries to fill urgent PA needs. Our team surfaces these before they're widely posted.

04

Tribal Health Programs

Tribally-operated programs offer unique practice environments, strong compensation, and loan repayment programs. Our team approaches these partnerships with care and respect for the communities involved.

05

Locum & Contract Positions

High-paying locum contracts are often the fastest path to top PA compensation. Our team actively sources short-term and long-term contract opportunities across all specialties and regions.

Locum Tenens vs. Permanent — The Pay Gap
Locum tenens PAs typically earn 20–30% more per hour than equivalent permanent employees, plus housing stipends and travel reimbursement on many assignments. Full-time locum positions average $203k–$210k annually based on current posted rates.
Source: AMN Healthcare · CompHealth 2025–2026
NHSC Loan Repayment Eligibility
Many facility types we source from — FQHCs, RHCs, CAHs, and Tribal Health programs — qualify for National Health Service Corps (NHSC) loan repayment. Our team flags these for eligible PAs. Eligibility requirements apply; visit HRSA.gov for current program details.
5-Year Compensation Growth
AAPA data shows consistent median compensation growth: $111k (2020) → $115k (2021) → $120k (2022) → $127k (2023) → $134k (2024) → $140k (2025). A 26% increase over five years.
Source: AAPA Annual Salary Reports 2020–2026
