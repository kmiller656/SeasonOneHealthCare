Who We Serve

The Facilities
We Partner With

Our particular focus is facilities in underserved and rural settings that are often overlooked by larger national staffing firms.

01

Critical Access Hospitals

Serve rural communities as the primary or sole source of acute care. Staffing these facilities requires professionals who are adaptable, scope-flexible, and genuinely committed to rural healthcare delivery. We understand those requirements and recruit accordingly.

02

Federally Qualified Health Centers

FQHCs provide care to underserved populations regardless of ability to pay. These organizations require professionals who are mission-aligned and understand the unique demands of community-based, patient-centered care.

03

Rural Health Clinics

Operate in designated healthcare shortage areas with specific staffing requirements for advanced practice providers. We are familiar with these regulatory requirements and work to place candidates who meet them.

04

Tribal Health & Community Clinics

Tribally-operated health programs and independent community clinics serve populations with distinct needs. We approach these partnerships with respect and take additional care to identify candidates who are culturally aware and community-committed.

05

Independent & Specialty Clinics

We also work with independent practices and specialty clinics that need permanent staffing support without the overhead of a large national agency relationship. If your facility has an open role, we'd like to talk.
