About Us

Why We Started
Season One Healthcare

We built Season One Healthcare because we believe healthcare facilities — especially those serving underserved communities — deserve a staffing partner that takes their mission seriously.

Our Story

Staffing With Purpose

Season One Healthcare was founded on the belief that the staffing process should be straightforward, honest, and built around what a facility actually needs — not just what is easiest to deliver.

We specialize in permanent direct placement, working with hospitals, health centers, and clinics to find professionals who are not only qualified but genuinely aligned with the communities they will serve. We take the time to understand each facility's culture, patient population, and operational needs before presenting a single candidate.

The name reflects where we are: this is our first season. We are building something deliberately, one placement and one relationship at a time.

"The right professional in the right role changes patient outcomes. That is the only standard we hold ourselves to."
Season One Healthcare — Our Standard

What We Stand For

Transparency

We are clear about our process, our timelines, and our limitations. No hidden steps, no surprise obligations.

Quality Over Volume

We present fewer, better candidates. We would rather take more time to find the right person than quickly send a list of mediocre options.

Relationship First

We invest in understanding our facility partners before we ever source a candidate. The better we know you, the better we can serve you.

Community Commitment

We prioritize placing professionals who are genuinely interested in serving the communities your facility is built to support.

Permanent Placement

We focus on direct hire placements that create lasting employment relationships between facilities and the professionals they need.

Thorough Vetting

Every candidate is screened for licensure, credentials, and alignment with your facility's specific needs before we make an introduction.

Simple Model

A one-time placement fee. No ongoing staffing contracts. No complicated obligations. Once placed, the relationship is yours.

Our Services

Built Specifically
for Physician Assistants

We focus exclusively on PA placement — permanent positions, contract work, and career support tailored to the unique needs of PA-Cs and PA students nationwide.

Permanent Placement

We identify, screen, and present PA candidates for permanent positions at facilities across the country. You interview, you decide, and the employment relationship is yours from day one.

Contract & Locum Work

Short-term and locum tenens opportunities for PAs who want flexibility without sacrificing clinical quality. We match you with facilities that have immediate or seasonal coverage needs.

Curated Job Listings

Personally curated job board updates sent directly to you — permanent roles, locum positions, and contract work filtered specifically for PAs. No noise, no roles that don't apply to you.

Rural & Underserved Placement

Specialized focus on Critical Access Hospitals, FQHCs, Rural Health Clinics, and Tribal Health programs — settings where PA practice scope is broad and the work is meaningful.

Candidate Pre-Screening

Every candidate is verified for active licensure, DEA eligibility, and clinical fit before we present them to a facility. Facilities receive only qualified, ready PAs.

PA Student Support

We work with PA students approaching graduation — helping you understand the job market, identify the right facilities, and position yourself for your first or next role before you finish your program.

Who We Serve

The Facilities
We Partner With

Our particular focus is facilities in underserved and rural settings that are often overlooked by larger national staffing firms.

01

Critical Access Hospitals

Critical Access Hospitals serve rural communities as the primary or sole source of acute care. Staffing these facilities requires professionals who are adaptable, scope-flexible, and genuinely committed to rural healthcare delivery. We understand those requirements and recruit accordingly.

02

Federally Qualified Health Centers

FQHCs provide care to underserved populations regardless of ability to pay. These organizations require professionals who are mission-aligned and understand the unique demands of community-based, patient-centered care. We work to find candidates who are the right fit, not just the right credential.

03

Rural Health Clinics

Rural Health Clinics operate in designated healthcare shortage areas and carry specific staffing requirements for advanced practice providers. We are familiar with these regulatory requirements and work to place candidates who meet them.

04

Tribal Health & Community Clinics

Tribally-operated health programs and independent community clinics serve populations with distinct needs and expectations. We approach these partnerships with respect for the communities involved and take additional care to identify candidates who are culturally aware and community-committed.

05

Independent & Specialty Clinics

We also work with independent practices and specialty clinics that need permanent staffing support without the overhead of a large national agency relationship. If your facility has an open role and needs a thoughtful staffing partner, we'd like to talk.
