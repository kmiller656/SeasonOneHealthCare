Our Difference

Our PA contact database is built for the independent recruiter, not just the enterprise team. You get direct, self-serve access to the full PA database — no seat minimums, no annual contracts, no sales calls required to start. Every contact is verified before you spend a credit revealing it, so your time goes toward outreach, not cleanup. Whether you're placing one PA a month or fifty, you get the same verified data and the same flat, transparent pricing.

Featured FAQs

How do I get started?+

Create an account and choose the plan that fits your volume. There's no sales call required — you can be searching the database within minutes.

How much does it cost?+

Plans will be flat-rate and priced by volume rather than by outcome. Subscriptions aren't open yet — create an account to be notified as soon as they are, or contact us with questions in the meantime.

How do I export contacts to my ATS or CRM?+

Every plan includes one-click CSV export. Download your filtered results and import them directly into your existing ATS, CRM, or outreach tool.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Monthly plans have no long-term commitment, and any unused credits never expire.
