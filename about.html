Your Career. Our Team. Your Terms.

Built around a simple belief: every Physician Assistant deserves a dedicated team working in their corner.

Built to Represent You

The PA job market is full of job boards, generic recruiters, and platforms built to serve facilities — not clinicians. PAs spend dozens of hours searching, applying, and negotiating alone, while the facilities they're applying to have HR departments, compensation consultants, and legal teams working on their side.

Season One Healthcare was founded by a PA with prior Navy enlisted service and an AEMT background to change that dynamic. Our agents work on your behalf — sourcing opportunities, vetting facilities, and identifying the highest-paying contracts and direct hire positions available across the United States so you don't have to go looking for them.

The name reflects where we are: this is our first season. We're building something deliberately, one PA and one placement at a time, with a commitment to always keeping our placement service free for the clinicians we represent.

We Work For You

Our team answers to you, not the facility. Your goals, your preferences, your terms drive everything we do.

Quality Over Volume

We don't flood your inbox. Every opportunity is curated and relevant before it reaches you.

Free, Always

Active PAs and PA students never pay for placement — not now, not at placement, not ever. Our fee comes from the facility.

Our Mission

Why We Also Built a PA Contact Database

Most recruiting databases treat Physician Assistants as an afterthought — a filter inside a general candidate pool built for every profession at once. Season One Healthcare built a single-purpose, PA-only contact database for healthcare recruiters and staffing firms who need to reach real PAs, not sort through noise.

Every contact is verified before it's revealed. Pricing is a flat subscription, not a per-placement fee. And tools like our Grant Tracker go beyond contact data to surface facilities with fresh funding and a real staffing need.
