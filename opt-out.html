Data Removal Request

If you are a Physician Assistant and you would like your contact information removed from our PA contact database, submit the form below. We will process your request within 5 business days and permanently suppress your information from the database and all future subscriber exports.

Who can submit this form? Any Physician Assistant (PA-C or PA student) who believes their professional contact information may be included in our PA contact database. You do not need to confirm that your information is in the database — we will check and process your request either way.

What happens after you submit? We will verify your request and permanently remove and suppress your email address from our PA contact database within 5 business days. You will receive a confirmation email when your request is processed. Your information will not appear in any future subscriber searches or exports.
